# coding_is_meditation

> **Coding Is Meditation** is a calm, first-principles, hand-holding introduction to computers, technology, and programming for beginners and everyday laptop users.

![alt text](banner.jpg)

* It builds deep understanding of how computers, software, websites, and systems actually work; without assuming any prior technical background.
* The course emphasizes clarity, structure, and intentional thinking, treating coding as a thought process rather than rote syntax.
* Learners develop strong tech awareness and production-grade habits instead of just following tutorials.
* By the end, a learner is not just a *coder* but a **developer**; capable of learning and upskilling independently, reading and understanding documentation, setting up environments, working with virtual environments, and confidently exploring new tools and technologies on their own.
